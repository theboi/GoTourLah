PROJECT BRIEF

Context

For this year, students are challenged to develop prototype-able solutions that would mitigate the adverse effects of the COVID-19 pandemic that reached its peak in May 2020, with some parts of the world still reeling from the aftermath.

Given the broad nature of the above theme and wide-ranging impact of the pandemic, some sub-themes are offered to narrow down the scope of the challenge.

On a similar note, some innovative ideas that have come out from our response to the pandemic have also been in the news. The clips below are just some that have been featured on public space.
