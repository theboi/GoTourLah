SPORTS & WELLNESS

IMPACT TO PUBLIC HEALTH

Another key industry that has been impacted significantly is the sports and wellness industry. With measures such as social distancing and others that seems limiting in nature, how can organisations that offer such services and experiences, or those that organise sporting competitions with mass participation, adapt to this new normal?

SOME QUESTIONS TO PONDER

  • How might we manage the organisation of public sporting events and use of corresponding venues more safely?

  • How might we encourage members of the public to still exercise whilst maintaining safety?

  • How might we reach make sports even more inclusive people like the elderly, the physically disabled and to those who rarely exercise, etc, in spite of the new normal?

  • How might we ensure the mental well being of our family members, friends and members of the public?
